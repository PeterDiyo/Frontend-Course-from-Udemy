Peter Diyo

Peter Diyo is a 17-year-old student from Chinika Secondary School in Lusaska, Zambia. He is a mathematics whiz who won the first prize in the 2022 National Junior Engineers, Technicians and Scientists (JETS) Fair. He also won the overall best student award at the fair. Peter is passionate about mathematics and science. He aspires to become a doctor and use his skills to help others. He is a role model for his peers and an inspiration to the people of Zambia.
